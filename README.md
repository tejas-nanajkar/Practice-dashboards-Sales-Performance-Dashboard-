# Sales-Performance-Dashboard-
A modern Sales Performance Dashboard that tracks revenue, order volume, regional performance, and product-wise sales. Designed for sales managers and business analysts to monitor real-time performance across channels and regions.

**Description**  
A clean and modern sales analytics dashboard designed to track revenue performance, regional contribution, product success, and channel effectiveness.

**Key Features & Visualizations:**

- **Executive KPI Cards**:
  - Total Sales: **253K**
  - Total Quantity: **261**
  - Total Orders: **150**
  - Average Order Value: **1.69K**

- **Sales by Channel** – Donut chart (Retail 54.74% | Online 45.26%)
- **Sales by Region** – Bar chart (East: 66K, West: 64K, North: 63K, South: 62K)
- **Sales by Product** – Horizontal bar chart (Tablet leads with 92K)
- **Interactive Filters** – Region, Product (Laptop, Mobile, Tab, Tablet), Channel, and Order Date

**Business Value:** Helps sales leaders quickly identify top-performing regions and products, optimize channel strategy, and monitor overall business health.
